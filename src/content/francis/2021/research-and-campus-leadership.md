Before industry internships, I built strong foundations through research and campus leadership.

Highlights:

- **Undergraduate Researcher, NSF-DART (2021):** Investigated disinformation techniques in data visualization, analyzed 40+ statistical data points, and produced a literature review.
- **Math Tutor (2021):** Supported college algebra students with one-on-one and supplemental instruction; most supported students finished with strong outcomes.
- **Resident Assistant (2021 onward during university):** Selected in a competitive process and helped drive student engagement initiatives.
- **President/Lead, Google Developer Student Club (2022-2024):** Co-led workshops, resume events, and speaker sessions that prepared students for technical careers.

These roles taught me how to combine technical depth with service, communication, and team coordination.
